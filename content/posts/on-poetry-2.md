<div style="text-align: right"><small>by Mark Ayers, 1986</small></div>

Poetry that's written\
To entertain oneself

Has a value of its own\
Just like a little elf

Who runs around\
And picks his toes\
And doesn't have a care

Who runs about\
Without clothes\
In the morning air.
