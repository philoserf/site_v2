- Answer:
  - Why are you writing this? What are your goals?
  - Who will read the document? Who might read it?
  - Who may be affected by the content?
  - What is the background?
- State your thesis.
- Write an outline.
- Write the first draft.
- Revise and polish, repeat.
- Publish.
